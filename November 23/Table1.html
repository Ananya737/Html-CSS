My book reading journey!

For me book reading is something that has changed my life completely. I still remember the first book that I've read. It was "The power of your subconscious mind". It led my life towards positivity and made me belief that we really can do and become whatever we want to. I started my reading journey with a non fiction book though I sometimes think had I started with fiction I could've developed interest in reading faster.
Over time I have realised that I enjoy reading fiction books more than non-fiction.
